Tom Brown Porridge Recipe

Description

Tom Brown is Ghana's very own cornflakes, well for adults and children anyway. For babies, it is perhaps one of Africa's most significant dishes, a meal for weaning babies that is packed with essential nutrients and easily affordable for all..

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ready made Tom Brown mix
  • 1-2 teaspoons of sugar to taste
  • 400ml of water
  • Water
  • 1-2 tablespoons of evaporated milk or double if fresh milk is used

Steps

  1. Add cold water to Tom Brown mix .
  2. Stir and put on high to medium heat .
  3. Continue to stare regularly as mix begins to thicken
  4. Turn the heat off after 10 minutes or as the Tom Brown begins to boil .
  5. Add sugar and milk to taste
