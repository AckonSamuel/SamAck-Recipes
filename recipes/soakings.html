Gari Soakings Recipe

Description

Gari soakings is one of the very easy ways out of hunger especially in the afternoons, or even mornings sometimes. It is very simple to make.

Gari is made from cassava that has been crushed, fermented and sieved into small pieces. These small pieces are then roasted or fried, and thatâ€™s when we get our well known gari.

Ingredients

  • Gari
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Roasted groundnuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Pour your preferred amount of gari into a bowl.
  2. Add sugar, groundnuts and water to it and stir.
  3. Add your milk and stir.
  4. Your gari is now ready to eat and you can add bread or biscuit to it.
